## How to Use the App

### 1) Open the app
Launch the app on your Android phone, tablet, or Android TV.

### 2) Log in
On the login screen:

1. Enter your name (for testing, enter `test`)
2. Tap **Login**

If login succeeds, you will be taken to the main channel screen.

### 3) Browse channels
On the main screen:

- Scroll to see available channels
- More channels load automatically as you scroll

### 4) Search channels
Use the search box to find channels quickly by name.

### 5) Filter channels
Use the **Group** and **Country** dropdown filters to narrow results.

### 6) Manage favourites
- Tap the favourite icon on a channel to add/remove it from favourites
- Tap the **Favourites** button to switch between all channels and favourite channels only

### 7) Watch a channel
Tap any channel card to open the player and start playback.

### 8) Log out
Tap the **Logout** button to end your session.

## Troubleshooting

### Login doesn’t work
- Check internet connection
- Try again after a few seconds
- If needed, request a personal account at [info@uplisoft.com](mailto:info@uplisoft.com)

### Channels don’t load
- Confirm internet is available
- Pull back and retry
- Restart the app

### Channel won’t play
- Try another channel
- Check network speed/stability
- Some streams may be temporarily unavailable
- For stable channels search **_km** and check all the **_km** channels
